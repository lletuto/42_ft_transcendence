FAQ - Rock, Paper, Scissors

Q: How do I play against the bot?
A: From the home page, click "Play" then "Against the bot". You start on level 0.

Q: How do I play against someoene?
A: From the home page, click "Play" then "Multiplayer".

Q: What is the Well?
A: It is a fourth move on top of Rock, Paper and Scissors. The Well beats Rock and Scissors but loses to Paper. It becomes available at level 2.

Q: Why did my score drop by 2 points?
A: From level 2 onward, losing a round while you played the Well costs 2 points instead of just losing the round. The score never goes below 0.

Q: How many points do I need to win?
A: On level 0 you need 5 points. On levels 1, 2 and 3 you need 10 points.

Q: How do combos work?
A: Playing three special winning moves in a row gives a bonus point. Level 1 has three-Rocks, three-Papers and three-Scissors. Level 3 adds extra patterns and a giga-combo for three Wells.

Q: How many difficulty levels does the bot have?
A: One

Q: Can I play against another player?
A: yes you Can

Q: Does the bot cheat?
A: No. The bot does not see your move. It only tries to predict your next move from your previous moves and the result of the last round.
