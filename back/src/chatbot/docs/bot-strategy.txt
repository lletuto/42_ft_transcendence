HOW TO BEAT THE AI BOT - TIPS & TRICKS

The bot is a prediction machine: it watches how you play, guesses your next
move, and plays whatever beats it. Every trick below exploits one of its habits.

--- TIP 1: BE RANDOM
The bot only wins when you are predictable. It looks for patterns in your moves.
If you play truly randomly, its predictions become useless and it cannot get an
edge. Repeating yourself is how you lose.

--- TIP 2: NEVER REPEAT THE MOVE THAT JUST WON ---
The bot assumes "win-stay, lose-shift": after you win a round, it expects you to
play the same move again, and after you lose it expects you to switch. So do the
opposite of the habit: change your move after a win, and feel free to keep one
after a loss. This breaks its read on you.

--- TIP 3: WATCH YOUR OWN SEQUENCES ---
The bot remembers what you usually play right AFTER a given move. If you always
follow Rock with Paper, it will catch that and counter it. Mix up your follow-ups
so "what comes after X" stays unpredictable.

--- TIP 4: PUNISH THE WELL WITH PAPER (LEVEL 2+) ---
From level 2 the Well is unlocked and the bot loves it, because the Well beats
both Rock and Scissors. But the Well has one weakness: Paper beats the Well.
When you feel the bot leaning on the Well, drop a Paper to punish it.

--- TIP 5: BLUFF WITH PAPER TO SCARE OFF THE WELL (LEVEL 2+) ---
The bot avoids the Well when it thinks you are about to play Paper. So if you
play Paper often, it gets scared and stops using its strongest move. Once it
stops fearing nothing, you are free to win with Rock or Scissors. Use Paper as
a threat, not just as an attack.

--- TIP 6: EXPLOIT ITS PANIC WHEN IT IS LOSING ---
When the bot falls behind on score it plays more seriously and takes bigger
risks with the Well. Knowing this, keep a Paper ready when you are ahead: a
losing bot is more likely to gamble on the Well, and Paper punishes it.

--- TIP 7: RELAX WHEN IT IS WINNING ---
When the bot is far ahead it plays more cautiously and avoids risky moves. It
becomes more conservative and easier to read, so a sudden change of rhythm from
you can catch it off guard and start a comeback.

--- LEVEL CHEAT SHEET ---
Level 0: no Well, first to 5. Easiest. Just avoid repeating yourself.
Level 1: no Well, first to 10. The bot doubles down on what works - vary more.
Level 2: Well unlocked, first to 10. Use Paper to punish and to bluff the Well.
Level 3: Well unlocked, first to 10, hardest. All tricks combined: stay random,
         keep Paper as your anti-Well weapon, and never settle into a pattern.
